# Groups and consensus

Participants are placed into groups based on their opinion.
- Participants who agree (or disagree) together on similar statements are grouped together.
  Groups represent contrasting ‘takes’ on the topic.

Finally we find statements that every group tends to agree on - these are consensus statements
- This is not a majority vote! Different groups are given equal weight regardless of their size, so smaller groups also have their voice heard
